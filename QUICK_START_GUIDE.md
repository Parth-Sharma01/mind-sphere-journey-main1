# 🎯 MindSphere Quick Start Guide

Welcome to your premium mental wellness platform! Here's how to get the most out of MindSphere.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. **Start Your Mind Score Assessment**

- Click "Begin Your Mind Score" on the home page
- Answer 30 scientifically-designed questions about your mental wellbeing
- Get scores across 4 key dimensions with personalized insights
- **Time:** ~10-15 minutes

**What You'll Learn:**

- Your Emotional Resilience
- Your Focus & Clarity
- Your Stress Balance
- Your Social Harmony

### 2. **Explore Your MeLodY OfLife**

- Navigate to "MeLodY OfLife" from the main menu
- Answer 3 thoughtful questions about your journey:
  1. Are you preparing for competitive exams?
  2. What's your deeper "why"?
  3. What's your true ambition?
- Get an encouraging, personalized message
- Your responses are saved for future reflection

### 3. **Check Your Dashboard**

- View your latest Mind Score results
- See progress over multiple assessments (charts)
- Quick access to all major features
- Motivational reminders about your journey

### 4. **Write in Your Private Journal**

- Click "New Entry" to start writing
- Your entries are encrypted and stored locally
- Search through past entries
- Delete entries anytime
- **Privacy:** No one but you can read your entries

### 5. **Play Mindful Games**

Choose from 3 scientifically-proven games:

**Flower Breathing** 🌸

- Visual breathing exercise
- Reduces anxiety naturally
- 5 rounds of 4-4-6 breathing pattern
- **Goal:** Calm your nervous system

**Stroop Test** 🎨

- Test your cognitive control
- 30-second challenge
- Select the color, not the word
- **Goal:** Measure focus and mental control

**Focus Maze** 🎯

- Navigate a mental maze
- Measure your patience and attention
- Click steps in order
- **Goal:** Build sustained focus

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I score low?**
A: Low scores show areas needing attention and growth. This is valuable information! Seek support, try new practices, and celebrate small improvements.

**Q: Can I retake the assessment?**
A: Yes! Each attempt uses different questions. Try weekly or monthly to track progress.

**Q: Are my entries really private?**
A: Yes. They're encrypted with a cipher and stored locally. No one can access them.

**Q: What if I clear my browser data?**
A: All your data (assessments, journal, game scores) will be deleted. Be careful!

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Currently saved locally only. Future versions may include export features.

**Q: Do the games really help?**
A: Yes. They're scientifically-based:

- Breathing: Proven to reduce anxiety
- Stroop: Measures cognitive control
- Maze: Builds attention and patience
